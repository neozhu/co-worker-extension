# Privacy Policy for My Co-worker Extension

**Last updated: July 8, 2025**

## Overview

This privacy policy explains how the "My Co-worker" browser extension handles information.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- **Personal browsing data**: We do not track or collect your browsing history
- **Personal information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal data
- **Usage analytics**: We do not monitor how you use the extension
- **Cookies or tracking**: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

## Information We DO Access

- **Employee search queries**: When you search for co-worker information, your search terms are sent to the authorized employee search service (employeesearchservice.voith.net) to provide results
- **Search results**: The extension displays results returned from the employee search service

## Data Storage

- **Local storage only**: Any data temporarily stored by the extension remains on your local device
- **No cloud storage**: We do not store or sync any data to remote servers
- **No data persistence**: Search queries and results are not permanently stored

## Third-Party Services

This extension connects to:
- **Employee Search Service** (employeesearchservice.voith.net): Used to retrieve employee information based on your search queries

## Data Security

- All connections to the employee search service use HTTPS encryption
- No data is transmitted to unauthorized third parties
- The extension operates within your browser's security sandbox

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension updates.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact your IT support team.

**Note**: This extension is designed for internal use within Voith organization and complies with company data protection policies.
